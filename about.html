About the Game

Number Guess

Number Guess is a fun game where players try to guess a randomly generated number within a certain range. It's a simple yet challenging game that tests your intuition and guessing skills.

Rules:

  • The game randomly selects a number within a specified range.
  • Players take turns guessing the number.
  • If a player's guess matches the random number, they win the game.
  • If all guesses are exhausted without a correct guess, the game is over.

Features:

  • Choose the range of numbers for the game.
  • Challenge yourself or play with friends.
  • Track your score and progress.
